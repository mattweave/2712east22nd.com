About This Home

A home that feels like it was assembled over time — rich, layered, and deeply considered. Unit 2 at 2712 E. 22nd Street is a newly built 4-bedroom, 3-bath residence where warmth and drama exist in perfect balance.

Arrive through a stained tongue-and-groove covered entry framed by custom lantern lighting and a dark mahogany arched front door — an immediate signal that what's inside is different. The exterior presents in Sherwin-Williams Urbane Bronze, a deep, earthy tone grounded by natural stone and board and batten detail.

Inside, engineered hardwood flows throughout every level, anchored by warm-toned trim and crisp crown molding in the principal rooms. The kitchen is the social heart of the home — cream perimeter cabinetry with decorative molded tops gives way to a richly stained dark island. White marble perimeter countertops and a contrasting soapstone island surface are finished with unlacquered brass hardware, a pot filler above the 36" Thor gas range, and globe pendant lights hung beneath decorative ceiling medallions. A dedicated pantry with dark stained cabinetry, butcher block countertops, and a 24" wine cooler completes the kitchen suite.

The living room centers on a 36" vented gas fireplace framed by a marble surround — clean, modern, and sculptural. Five floating shelves flank the firebox.

The downstairs guest bath is a showstopper — black and white marble tile laid in a diamond pattern, a console-style vanity with champagne bronze legs, and a curbless shower clad to the ceiling. Upstairs, the primary bathroom is spa-grade: a 59" freestanding soaking tub with a deck-mounted champagne bronze filler, dual undermount sinks in a 96" vanity, a full-height marble shower with built-in bench, and an encaustic-patterned shower floor. The primary closet features a chandelier, built-out cubbies, butcher block island, and dedicated hanging sections.

Step outside to a covered back patio with a tongue-and-groove ceiling overlooking the private 8′ × 15′ plunge pool — a composed, sun-drenched retreat in the heart of East Austin.
